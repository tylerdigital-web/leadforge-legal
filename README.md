Privacy Policy — LeadForge Threads Tool

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LeadForge Threads Tool ("the App") collects, uses, and handles information when connected to a Threads account via the Meta Threads API.

1. Information We Collect

The App accesses the following information, strictly limited to what is authorized by the connected Threads account owner:


Own account content: posts, replies, and basic profile information belonging to the account that has authorized the App.
Public content: publicly available Threads posts retrieved through keyword search or public profile lookup, used solely to identify relevant content for engagement.
Engagement data: replies received on the account's own posts, and mentions of the account, used to generate response suggestions.


The App does not access private messages, private profiles, or any non-public data belonging to third parties.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used exclusively to:


Generate and publish content on behalf of the authorized account owner.
Identify publicly available posts relevant to the account's subject matter, for the purpose of composing replies.
Generate suggested replies using an AI language model, based on the text of public posts or comments.


No information is used for advertising, resold, or shared with any third party other than the AI processing service (OpenAI) used solely to generate text content. OpenAI does not receive any data beyond the specific post or comment text needed to generate a single response, and does not receive access to the Threads account itself.

3. Data Retention


Access tokens are stored securely and used only to perform actions authorized by the account owner.
Post and reply text used to generate content is processed transiently and is not retained beyond what is necessary to complete the action (publishing a post or reply).
The App does not maintain a permanent database of third-party users' public content or profiles beyond short-term operational use.


4. Data Deletion

Account owners may revoke the App's access at any time through their Threads account settings. Upon revocation or deauthorization, any stored access token associated with the account is deleted.

To request deletion of any data associated with your account, contact us at the email address below.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share collected information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information is only shared with the AI processing service (OpenAI) as described in Section 2, solely for the purpose of generating text content.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect access tokens and account information from unauthorized access, including restricting storage to the automation platform used to operate the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
